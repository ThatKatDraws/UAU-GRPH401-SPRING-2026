Saint Motel

Indie / Band / Indie Pop

Saint Motel is an American indie pop band formed in Los Angeles in 2007, known for their vibrant visuals and cinematic sound. Fronted by A.J. Jackson, the band blends catchy hooks with orchestral elements and retro-inspired production. They gained widespread attention with hits like “My Type” and the album saintmotelevision, which showcased their flair for storytelling and multimedia creativity.
